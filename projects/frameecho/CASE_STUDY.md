# FrameEcho — Case Study

## Summary

FrameEcho is a duplicate video finder concept focused on identifying visually similar or duplicate media files based on content rather than filename alone.

## Problem

Duplicate videos often survive simple file-name or file-size checks because they may be renamed, recompressed, trimmed, or downloaded from different sources.

## Solution

FrameEcho is designed around content-based comparison: frame sampling, perceptual hashes, similarity scoring, and review workflows.

## Portfolio Value

FrameEcho is a strong technical concept because it demonstrates algorithmic thinking, utility, and a clear real-world problem.
