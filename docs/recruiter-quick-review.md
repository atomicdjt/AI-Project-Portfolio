## What This Portfolio Demonstrates

- Practical initiative: turning ambiguous ideas into runnable applications and reviewable case studies.
- Product judgment: naming users, workflows, limitations, and next improvements.
- Frontend implementation: React/Vite apps with local state, Canvas workflows, PDF/report generation, and responsive UI.
- Responsible scope: local-first privacy design, no exaggerated automation claims, and clear non-goals.
- Documentation discipline: recruiter guidance, technical explanations, project indexing, and validation notes.

## What This Portfolio Does Not Claim

- It does not claim senior engineering experience.
- It does not claim regulated compliance approval, legal advice, medical advice, financial advice, or fraud determinations.
- It does not claim every concept project is implemented as a runnable app.
- It does not claim live backend production systems unless documented in the specific project.
