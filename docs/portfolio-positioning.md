# Portfolio Positioning

## Best One-Line Description

A portfolio of AI-assisted applications, research tools, workflow systems, and product prototypes demonstrating technical operations judgment, product thinking, and structured documentation.

## Strong Public Profile Summary

I build AI-assisted tools and prototypes that turn complex workflows into structured, usable systems. My work combines product thinking, technical documentation, research synthesis, and practical frontend implementation across operations, education, bioinformatics, and digital-product concepts.

## What to Avoid Claiming

Avoid overstating the portfolio as senior-level software engineering work. The strongest honest framing is:

- AI-assisted product prototyping
- Technical operations systems
- Documentation and knowledge workflow design
- Research synthesis and structured analysis
- Early-stage frontend application development

## Best Portfolio Narrative

The portfolio should communicate: “I may not have a traditional software engineering background, but I can learn quickly, structure ambiguous problems, use AI intelligently, document clearly, and build practical prototypes that demonstrate real product judgment.”
