# FocusForge – AI-Assisted Productivity & Focus System

## Concept
FocusForge is a conceptual AI-assisted productivity system designed to help a user stay focused on meaningful work through coordinated AI "modes" (planner, summarizer, accountability assistant).

## Goals
- Turn an abstract desire for "an AI that keeps me focused" into a concrete, multi-mode workflow.
- Show how prompt design shapes user experience in a productivity context.

## Key Design Ideas
- Planner mode: breaks big goals into tasks and sequences them.
- Summarizer mode: condenses work sessions and surfaces next actions.
- Accountability mode: checks in on progress in a supportive, non-intrusive way.

## What This Demonstrates
- Systems thinking for GenAI products.
- Role-based prompting and content design.
- User-centered framing of AI interactions.
