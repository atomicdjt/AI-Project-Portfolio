# AI-Assisted PRISMA-Style Systematic Review – Dairy & Autoimmunity

## Overview
Independent project using multi-step prompt engineering to orchestrate an AI-assisted, PRISMA-style synthesis of the relationship between bovine dairy and autoimmune disease.

## Goals
- Move from a vague health question to a structured, academic-style research synthesis.
- Cover multiple autoimmune conditions and mechanistic pathways.
- Surface contradictions and funding bias in the literature.

## What I Did
- Designed a three-stage prompt sequence: feasibility scan, structured evidence synthesis, and standards escalation to PRISMA-style methods.
- Guided the model to define inclusion/exclusion criteria, group studies by disease subtype, and explicitly call out gaps, contradictions, and industry funding bias.
- Iterated on prompts to improve neutrality, transparency, and reproducibility of the methods section.

## Why It Matters
- Shows advanced prompt engineering beyond single-shot prompts.
- Demonstrates comfort working with complex, high-stakes domains and keeping tone balanced.
- Produces an output that approaches the standard of a publishable manuscript.
