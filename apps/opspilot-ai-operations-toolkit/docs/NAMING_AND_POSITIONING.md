# Naming and Search Positioning

Working product name: **OpsPilot Pro**

Primary positioning:

- Operations documentation workspace for small businesses.
- SOP builder, onboarding checklist builder, knowledge base generator, documentation gap detector, version tracker, audit log, and export dashboard.
- Deterministic demo by default, optional server-side AI generation when configured.

Search phrases to support in README/case-study language:

- AI operations toolkit
- SOP generator for small business
- Operations documentation software
- Training checklist builder
- Knowledge base generator
- Documentation gap detector
- Audit-ready operations docs

Avoid overclaiming:

- Do not call the public demo a production AI agent.
- Do not claim durable production storage until a real database adapter is connected.
- Do not claim real authentication until sessions are derived server-side from an identity provider.
- Do not market generated SOPs as legal, medical, HR, compliance, or safety advice.
